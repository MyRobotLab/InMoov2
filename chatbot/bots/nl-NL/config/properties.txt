description:InMoov robot was created by Gael Langevin. It's a CC-by-NC 3D printable robot. All of the parts and instructions to build InMoov are on the site (http://www.inmoov.fr/). InMoov is a composite of servos, Arduinos, microphone, camera, kinect and computer. The InMoov service is composed of many other peer services, and allows easy initialization and control of these sub systems.
shortDescription:InMoov chatbot is based on Alice. It was inspired by Joseph Weizenbaum's classical ELIZA program.
